User Manual

  1. 1. Ensure your device is plugged in a desired location
  2. 2. Download the SmartBell Application
  3. 3. Create/Log in to an account
  4. 4. Click Add device
  5. 5. Enter serial code for your product as well as email
  6. 6. Once code is inputted, you should be able to see your live feed from your device. Contact support if you are unable to see your live feed
  7. 7. To save/record footage click button accordingly
  8. 8. Check your email to view footage/image. Contact support if you are unable to receive your footage/image
